⚡ How It Works

From sensor to insight
in milliseconds.

A simple pipeline that transforms raw physiological data into actionable emotional intelligence.

01

Collect

The AuraLink neckband continuously monitors heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), body temperature, and galvanic skin response (GSR).

02

Classify

Our ML classifier analyzes the physiological patterns against calibrated baselines to determine the most likely emotional state with a confidence score.

03

Notify

If concerning states are detected (high stress, sustained anxiety), caregivers receive immediate push notifications with full context.

04

Analyze

Historical data is visualized in rich charts showing emotional distributions, daily stress trends, and correlation with activities and environment.
